Our Story

In the Beginning

Horn Aviation Logistics was conceptualized in 2017 when we realized that clients were in need of a consistent provider in air transport solution in Juba, South Sudan. There was a need for clients to harmonize their air operations into one company that could guarantee
✦ Safe and reliable aircraft operations.
✦ Consistent availability of aircraft solutions.
✦ Stable pricing of services.
✦ Credit facility through trusted partnerships.

Our Commitments

With our objectives identified we committed to the following solutions
✦ Have Standard Operating Procedures that exemplify safe operations.
✦ Operate serviceable and airworthy aircrafts if not new.
✦ Generate a central pool of aircrafts for clients to pick from.
✦ Have work contracts in place.
